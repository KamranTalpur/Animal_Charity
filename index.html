Our Mission

We are dedicated to improving the lives of animals through rescue, rehabilitation, and education.

Paws & Care was founded in 2010 with a simple goal: to create a world where every animal is treated with compassion and respect. Over the years, we've rescued thousands of animals from neglectful and abusive situations, provided them with medical care and rehabilitation, and found them loving forever homes.

Our work extends beyond rescue operations. We run educational programs in schools and communities to promote responsible pet ownership and advocate for animal welfare legislation. We believe that through education and direct action, we can create lasting change for animals everywhere.

We operate entirely on donations and the dedication of our volunteers. Every contribution, whether time or money, helps us continue our vital work.

Our Programs

We run several initiatives to help animals in need and promote animal welfare.

Rescue & Rehabilitation

We rescue animals from abusive situations and provide them with medical care, behavioral training, and a safe environment to recover.

Adoption Services

We match rescued animals with loving families through a careful screening process to ensure lifelong compatibility.

Education & Outreach

We conduct workshops and seminars in schools and communities to promote responsible pet ownership and animal welfare.
